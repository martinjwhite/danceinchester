Why Choose Dawn?

“Dawn is a former high level international competitor and has trained in Ballroom, Salsa and Tango with some of the world's top dance coaches.

Dawn is a prominent member of the Dance Matrix, Club Dance Holidays, HF Holidays and Warner Holidays teaching teams, and is qualified and recognised by the British and World Dance Councils as a professional teacher and adjudicator.

She teaches with a gentle, confidence building and supportive approach, underpinned by considerable experience and expertise, but one that is always first and foremost fun.”

Paul Bottomley, Former World Dance Champion
Four times World and European Supreme Champion
Director of The Dance Matrix
